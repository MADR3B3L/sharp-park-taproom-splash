Our story

A neighborhood spot, built by the neighborhood

Sharp Park Taproom was opened by Pacifica locals Kris Romo and Alex Englund with one goal: skip the big distributors and pour what local breweries are actually making, in a room built for hanging out. The mural behind the bar is hand-painted by Kris. The bar itself is 17 feet of ash wood. The dogs are welcome on the patio, the board games are out, and the game's usually on.
